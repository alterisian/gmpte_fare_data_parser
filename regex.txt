^\s*(\d+)((?: \d+)*)(\D+)[(]0?\1[)]$
matches all stage lines
------------------------------------------
e.g.
Result 6

   1. 6
   2. 150 150 150 130 90
   3. PLATT LN

Match 1: Stage Number
Match 2: All Fare Data space seperated
Match 3: Stage Name
------------------------------------------
^
  start of line
\s*
  zero or more whitespace characters
(\d+)
  an integer
((?: \d+)*)
  multiple integers with leading space
  ?: indicates don't capture the internal region
(\D+)
  one or more non-digits
[(]0?\1[)]$
  repeat the 1st match with optional leading zero. $ is end of line
